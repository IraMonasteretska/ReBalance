IPL Photofacial

The growing popularity and demand for photo-facial therapy have led to various innovations and applications of IPL for skin rejuvenation. Our doctors strive to keep up with the latest skincare technologies to deliver better therapeutic results in line with new safety profiles.

Our specialists use intense pulsed light to safely and effectively treat a diverse array of dermatological conditions, including skin pigmentation, vascular lesions, and acne scars. Depending on your needs, we can also provide comprehensive therapeutic options for facial rejuvenation and other skin conditions.

What is IPL Photofacial?

An IPL photofacial is a type of laser skin treatment that uses light to get under the surface of your skin, smoothing it out and reducing discoloration. IPL laser treatment works by lightening areas of discoloration with Intense Pulsed Light and creating new collagen under the epidermis to improve skin texture and create smooth skin. It usually takes several laser facial sessions spaced a few weeks apart to achieve the full effect of the IPL face treatment, and you should continue your holistic skincare regimen to ensure maximum benefits.

What conditions are
IPL Photofacials Used to Treat?

IPL photofacial (laser facial rejuvenation) uses Intense Pulsed Light (IPL) to correct a number of common skin conditions, including:

  • Age spots (sun spots)
  • Mild to moderate acne scarring
  • Melasma
  • Rosacea
  • Fine lines and wrinkles (Rhytides)
  • Large pores
  • Broken capillaries or spider veins
  • Stretch marks

How is the IPL Photofacial Treatment Performed?

The IPL facial NYC procedure (photorejuvenation) is minimally invasive and performed on an outpatient basis at our skin rejuvenation clinic. The Midtown office is designed for comfort, and you’ll relax in a soothing, spa-like atmosphere while your skin is treated with the best IPL treatment.

You’ll be given special eyewear to protect your delicate eyes from the light used during the procedure, and the clinician uses a handheld device to focus high energy beams of light on problem areas.

There’s usually little to no pain involved during the IPL laser treatment and the recovery time is minimal. You may experience mild swelling, inflammation, and discomfort following an IPL photofacial treatment, but these usually pass quickly and can often be helped by icing down the area or applying a cooling gel to the IPL skin treatment area before the procedure begins.

Maintaining IPL Treatment Results

After your IPL laser facial rejuvenation is complete, you can help maintain the results by continuing to practice conscientious care of your body, mind, and spirit. Remember to stay well-hydrated by drinking plenty of water and other healthful beverages such as green tea and matcha.

Beauty sleep isn’t a myth. Staying well-rested can minimize the appearance of dark circles under your eyes and help you maintain the energy necessary to stay active, which in turn helps to keep you healthy. Eat well and practice self-care, making time for rest, relaxation and plenty of time with loved ones. Regardless of the laser skin treatment you choose, the finishing touch is always a positive, happy glow created by living well.

Tightening Up with IPL Photofacials

As you get older, the effects of gravity kick in and can make your skin loose and a little saggy, especially around your cheeks and neck. This can draw down the corners of your mouth and eyes, giving you the appearance of being sad or upset when you really aren’t. Many people are turning to IPL therapy for skin tightening, a quick and usually painless laser skin care procedure in NYC that firms up the skin and gives you a younger, more upbeat appearance.

Like the IPL photorejuvenation, laser skin tightening works under the skin. The focused beams of high energy light heat up the collagen under the epidermis, causing the IPL skin treatment area to tighten. Any discomfort is typically offset by the use of a topical anesthetic cream applied before the IPL treatment procedure, and recovery time is minimal. It usually takes three sessions to see full results, and each photo facial session can last from a few minutes to about an hour, depending on how large an area needs to be treated.

How Much Does IPL Treatment Cost?

The cost may vary depending on your location, the type of condition, and the size of the treated area. The average cost of treatment per session can range from $ 700 to $ 1200. Although the therapy results are long-lasting, you may need to repeat the procedure once a year, which also affects the total cost.

What Are the Side Effects of
IPL Cosmetic Procedure?

To achieve the clinical effect of the treatment, the light must be absorbed by the tissues, which can lead to tender skin and temporary discomfort. Following the treatment you may experience the following side effects:

  • Slight bruising. The bruising normally dissipates after 1-2 weeks.
  • Reddened or sore skin. It can be treated with ice or topical ointment
  • Feeling of mild sunburn or flaky skin. Such side-effects are common and generally disappear after a few days.

After recovering from side effects, most of our patients notice an improvement in the appearance and condition of their skin.

What Are the Benefits of IPL?

In addition to improved self-image and boosted confidence, there are many substantial benefits. Photofacial laser treatment can help you to:

  • Tighten pores and skin
  • Make fine lines and wrinkles less noticeable
  • Reduce visibility of acne, sun spots, freckles, and scars
  • Treat vascular lesions
  • Cure skin flushing, redness, and sun damage
  • Improve the overall contour of the face

In addition to these benefits, the therapy boosts collagen and elastin production. As a result, your skin becomes firmer, healthier, and more protected against various environmental factors.
